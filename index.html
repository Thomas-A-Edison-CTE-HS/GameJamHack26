Team Roles

Everyone is essential. Roles are about balance, not ranking.

Team Captain

Keeps the team on track. Makes sure everyone is heard, decisions get made, and the prototype is playable.

Focus: planning, time, teamwork

Gameplay Architect

Defines rules, goals, and core mechanics. Makes sure the game is understandable and fun to playtest.

Focus: mechanics, rules, balance

Experience Builder

Builds the prototype: board layout, cards, UI, interactions. Turns ideas into something playable fast.

Focus: building, iteration, clarity

G4C Theme: Outgrow Hunger

Connect local ecosystems to global food systems.

Design Prompt

Design a game where players nurture a natural space that feeds a community. Show how everyday actions(planting, protecting, sharing) connect local ecosystems to global food systems, proving that small choices can fight hunger and build resilience.

Impact Lens
  • Show cause-and-effect (systems thinking).
  • Make scarcity real (trade-offs).
  • Teach through gameplay, not lectures.
Starter Questions
  • What resource is scarce?
  • Who benefits and who gets left out?
  • How does the ecosystem support food access?

Build Flow

Follow these steps. Don‚Äôt skip playtesting.

1) Brainstorm

  • Pick a player role
  • Pick a scarce resource
  • Define one trade-off

2) Prototype

  • Build the smallest playable version
  • Write simple rules
  • Add a win/lose condition

3) Playtest

  • Watch players
  • Note confusion
  • Ask: ‚ÄúWhat did you learn?‚Äù

Iteration Checklist

  • Remove one confusing rule
  • Make the goal clearer
  • Increase the trade-off
  • Shorten setup time
  • Make feedback visible (scores/resources)
  • Improve theme connection through mechanics
